Chipotle Fire Roasted Salsa

Ingredients

  1. Cherry Tomatoes
  2. 1 White onion
  3. 5-8 garlic cloves
  4. 1-5 chipotle peppers
  5. olive oil
  6. salt & pepper (to taste)
  7. The juice of 1 lime
  8. pinch Cayenne
  9. pinch of Dry oregano
  10. pinch of cumin
  11. cilantro leaves

Instructions for prep

Put all the cherry tomatoes, chipotle peppers, chopped onion and garlic in their skins on a lightly oiled roasting pan. Put the roasting pan in the oven under the broiler set to high, get the pan as close as possible to the broiler as you want the veggies to be blackened! Then place the broiled vegetables into a blender or food processor, and add the dry ingredients and cilantro leaves plus and left over juices from the frying pan. Blend it well! That's it!
